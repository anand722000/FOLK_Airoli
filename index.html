  • Aim of a Folk Residency: It is said that by taking shelter of pure devotees who have taken shelter of Krishna, one can become free of all the sinful tendencies and thus become eligible to perform devotional service. Such is the power of the association of pure devotees.
  • Srila Prabhupada says: One should learn how to associate with the devotees of the Lord by gathering with them to chant the glories of the Lord. This process is the most purifying. As devotees thus develop their loving friendship, they feel mutual happiness and satisfaction. And by thus encouraging one another, they can give up material sense gratification, which is the cause of all suffering.
